## 1. 搭建场景

> 中国儿童和尚,穿橘色的衣服,双手合十,{寺院外,菩提树下,寺院内},正面,微笑

> Chinese child monk, wearing orange clothes, hands folded, {temple, under the Bodhi tree, inside the temple}, facing, smiling
